About 3rd January

3rd January is a mental health awareness initiative created to remind people that no matter how the year starts, it is never too late to begin again. Mental health matters every day — not just on special dates.

Our Mission

Awareness

Breaking stigma and encouraging open conversations about mental health.

Support

Providing guidance, hope, and direction to those struggling.

New Beginnings

Helping people understand that healing can start any day — even on the 3rd of January.

Mental Health Resources

If you or someone you know needs help, please reach out to trusted professionals or local helplines.

  • Talk to someone you trust
  • Seek professional counseling
  • Practice self-care and rest
  • Remember: you are not alone
